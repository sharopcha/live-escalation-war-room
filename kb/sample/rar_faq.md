# RAR (Registrul Auto Român) — Vehicle Registration FAQ
# Public domain data — Romanian Auto Registry common questions

## Vehicle Registration

**Q: What documents are needed to register a vehicle in Romania?**
A: Required documents:
- Identity document (CI/passport)
- Vehicle purchase contract or transfer deed (translated and apostilled if foreign)
- Technical inspection certificate (ITP) — valid, issued within 90 days
- Compulsory insurance (RCA) — valid at time of registration
- Proof of payment of local tax (impozit auto)
- Completed registration form (downloadable from RAR website)

**Q: How long does vehicle registration take?**
A: Standard processing: 10-15 working days. Express service available at select RAR offices: 24-48 hours (additional fee applies).

**Q: Can I register a vehicle imported from the EU?**
A: Yes. EU vehicles require: EC Certificate of Conformity or individual approval, foreign deregistration certificate (COC), customs clearance for non-EU origin vehicles, and valid ITP from a Romanian authorised inspector.

## Technical Inspection (ITP)

**Q: How often is ITP required?**
A: 
- New vehicles: first ITP after 3 years, then annually
- Vehicles > 12 years old: every 6 months
- Commercial vehicles (>3.5t): every 6 months
- Motorcycles: annually after first inspection

**Q: What are common reasons for ITP failure?**
A: Top failure reasons: brake system defects, lighting failures, excessive exhaust emissions, tyre wear below legal minimum (1.6mm), steering play exceeding limits, suspension component wear.

**Q: My vehicle failed ITP. Can I drive it?**
A: No. A failed ITP means the vehicle is not roadworthy. You have 30 days to repair the identified defects and return for a re-inspection (fee applies for re-test). Driving with a failed/expired ITP carries fines of 400-800 RON.

## Document Replacement

**Q: How do I replace a lost vehicle registration certificate?**
A: Visit your local RAR / tax office (DITL) with: ID, police report confirming loss (declaratie de furt/pierdere), and payment of replacement fee (approx. 50 RON). Processing: 5-10 working days.

**Q: Can I request documents online?**
A: Partial online services are available at ghiseul.ro for payment of taxes. Physical presence required for registration, ITP scheduling, and document replacement.

## Refund & Payment Policy

**Q: Can I get a refund on vehicle registration tax?**
A: Vehicle registration tax (taxa de înmatriculare) is non-refundable once the registration certificate is issued. Refunds are only available if the registration was rejected due to RAR error.

**Q: What happens if I overpay my vehicle tax?**
A: Overpayments are refundable within 5 years. Submit a written request with supporting payment receipts to the local tax authority (DITL). Processing time: 45 days.
