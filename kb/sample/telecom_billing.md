# Telecom Billing & Refund Policy — Demo Knowledge Base

## Standard Refund Policy

Customers are eligible for a full credit or refund in the following cases:

- **Billing error**: Incorrect charge applied to account not matching contracted tariff → full credit, no approval needed for amounts ≤ €50
- **Service outage**: Verified network outage lasting >4 hours in billing period → pro-rated credit automatically applied
- **Double charge**: Same invoice line billed twice → full refund of duplicate amount, processed within 3 business days
- **Cancelled service still billed**: Charge appearing after confirmed cancellation → full refund + €10 goodwill credit

## Hardship Refunds (Require Supervisor Approval)

Hardship refund requests exceed standard policy. These must be reviewed by a supervisor:

- Requests > €50 in a single billing cycle
- Requests citing financial hardship, unemployment, or disability
- Requests from customers with outstanding balance > €100
- Any refund for a period older than 90 days

**Process**: Agent offers callback within 2 business hours. Supervisor reviews account history and authorises via the case management system.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: My bill is higher than usual this month. What should I do?**
A: We'll review your usage breakdown. Common causes include roaming charges, premium SMS, or a tariff change. If there's a billing error, we'll apply a credit immediately for amounts ≤ €50.

**Q: I was charged after I cancelled my subscription.**
A: This qualifies for a full refund. We'll process it within 3 business days and add a €10 goodwill credit to your next invoice.

**Q: The internet was down for 2 days. Can I get compensation?**
A: Yes. Verified outages of >4 hours qualify for a pro-rated credit. For a 2-day outage on a monthly plan, that's approximately 6.5% of your monthly fee credited automatically.

**Q: I can't afford my bill this month.**
A: We understand. Payment plans are available for balances up to €200. Hardship refund requests above €50 require supervisor approval — we'll call you back within 2 business hours.
